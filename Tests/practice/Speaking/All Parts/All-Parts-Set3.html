Question 1 of 5
🎬 Cinema vs Home

What are the differences between watching a movie at home and in a cinema?

Consider: atmosphere, cost, convenience, sound and picture quality, social experience.

🎬 Future of Cinema

Do you think cinemas will still be popular in the future?

Think about: streaming services, ticket prices, the cinema experience, new technologies like VR.

🎭 Media Influence

Do movies have a strong influence on the way people think?

Consider: values, stereotypes, social norms, political messages, cultural awareness.

🏛️ Government & Arts

Should the government provide financial support to the local film industry?

Think about: cultural heritage, job creation, competition with foreign films, economic return.

📽️ Film Industry Changes

How have movies changed in your country over the last few decades?

Consider: themes, technology, budgets, international influence, audience tastes.
